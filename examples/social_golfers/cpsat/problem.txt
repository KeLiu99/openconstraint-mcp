Social Golfer Problem (7-3-10): schedule 21 golfers into 7 groups of 3 over 10 weeks so no two golfers share a group more than once. This CP-SAT example uses a compact Fano-plane construction: week 1 fixes the seven base groups, and weeks 2-10 choose permutations for each base group so every cross-group pair code is all-different. The script runs OR-Tools CP-SAT locally with a 300 second solver budget, portfolio quick restarts, randomized search, symmetry detection, and LNS parameters enabled.
